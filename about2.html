Makeup/Hair/Accessories

These styles is where you can be creative. You are able to have cool hair styles as well as fun makeup other then your regular competition makeup and you will sometimes have accessories around your neck, arms, legs etc. During competitions you will notice that the judges will even comment on those things and how they love it because it is different then what they normally see people wear. Not everyone on your team will be wearing the same outfit either. sometimes your teacher will want to switch it up and have your whole team in three different types of costumes. How to create a dance

  1. Pick a song
  2. Start creating choreography
  3. Start teaching it to the class slowly
  4. Figure out positioning of who will be standing where
  5. Count the steps out for the class
  6. Have the students go over what they had just learned multiple times before adding on

Why are these styles my favorite?

While performing these two styles there is nothing you can do but have a smile on your face. Whatever mood you are in it will change instantly. These styles are when you can let loose and be fun while interacting with your teammates. The music that you dance to can range from remixes of different songs to get you up and moving. People tend to find these two styles the most entertaining. When it comes to jazz and hip hop you are also the most out of breath when finishing the dance.
